# Changelog

## v1.0 — May 2026

Initial release of the TRACE Content Accountability Standard.

**Framework**
- Five-condition standard: Transparency, Representation, Accountability, Consent, Engagement
- Track A (Community Insider, 20 pts) — six categories for self-funded, non-commercial community content
- Track B (External/Institutional, 35 pts) — seven categories for external, commercial, or AI-assisted content, adding Structural Benefit and Data Rights

**Documents**
- Community Content Compact — foundation document, open for endorsement
- Policy brief — *Who Gets to Tell a Community's Story?* — for funders and policymakers

**Tools**
- Interactive scorecard with shareable accountability cards
- Deployed at tracestandard.org via Cloudflare Workers
